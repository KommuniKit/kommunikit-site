Make it your own

No two communities are the same.
Your online space shouldn't be either.

KommuniKit is a tool for program coordinators and community leaders. Think of it like moving into a new space and making it your own — you can move the furniture around, redecorate, and set it up to suit exactly how your community works. Intuitive to configure, and genuinely enjoyable for members to use.

What's included

Turn on the features you need.
Don't pay for the ones you'll never use.

KommuniKit comes with six content types. Each one is optional and configurable — so your platform only has what your community actually needs.

Member Directory

Rich, filterable profiles that help people actually find each other. Browseable flip-cards make discovering fellow members genuinely enjoyable.

AI-powered

Magic Connector

Reads your members' profiles and surfaces the right introductions — so the most valuable connections in your network don't go unmade. Works across communities of any size.

Posts & Announcements

Peer-to-peer posts for member discussions and stories, plus a separate announcements space for official updates — so your members never miss the news that matters, and the two never get tangled together.

Events

Calendar view, RSVPs, and Add to Calendar — all in one place. Events that members can find, remember, and actually show up to.

Resource Library

A browseable collection of guides, reports, toolkits, and links that your community can actually find — not buried in a thread from six months ago.

Marketplace

Member-to-member offers, services, and asks. A lightweight way to put your community's collective expertise and opportunities to work.
